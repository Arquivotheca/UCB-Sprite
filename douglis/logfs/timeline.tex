\caption{Time-line for migration of a process touching 25 8K-byte heap
pages, having
three open file descriptors.  The
dominating costs are  for transferring the stack, heap, and open
files of the process.  The entire operation takes approximately 560
milliseconds.  All times listed are real time; CPU time was not
measured.  (Note: the resolution of the Sun-3 timer chip is 10
milliseconds, which is too long an interval to obtain accurate values
for the time taken at each step.   The time-line is therefore a
composite average of 10 identical migrations.)
